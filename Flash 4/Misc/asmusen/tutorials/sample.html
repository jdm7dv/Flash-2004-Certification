Oh, and to you guys who wants to get quickly started by making 3D in Flash, here's a technique (old but good):

1. Get Xara3D at http://www.xara.com/xara3D , a program that quickly allows you to make 3D animated gifs by any installed font.

2. Make your 3D in Xara, i guarantee it's easy.

3. Export as an animated GIF.

4. Import in Flash.

5. Trace every BITMAP, by clicking "Modify > Trace Bitmap".

6. With a little patience, these bitmaps can be optimized.

7. Select the curve you wish to optimize and press CTRL + ALT + SHIFT + C. This will simplify the curve.

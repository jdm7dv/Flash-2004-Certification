WinGroove

WinGroove is a software wavetable synthesizer. This means, a piece of software, that enables you to play MIDI files with a great better quality than that of the standard MIDI player. Basically, it is as good as buying a SoundBlaster AWE 32! It can be downloaded freely as a 30-day trial here: http://www.cc.rim.or.jp/~hiroki/english/

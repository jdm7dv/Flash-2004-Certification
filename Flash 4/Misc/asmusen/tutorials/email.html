Creating an email button

printable version

It is possible to create a button, that opens an email-composer from the users email program.

Step 1. Create the button

Step 2. Assign a GET URL command with the following:

mailto:yourname@server.com

Step 3. For the advanced user, you may also assign a subject:

mailto:yourname@server.com?subject=Hello%20There!

In this case, %20 equals a space.

That's it!

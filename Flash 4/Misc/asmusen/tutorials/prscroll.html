Making Scrollable Text

printable version

Downloadable FLA

Ever wanted to make text you could scroll in Flash? Well, it's possible! The way forward is not obvious, but very easy when you understand.

Step 1. Write the text you want to be able to scroll.

Step 2. Select the text and press F8 and make it a movie clip.

Step 3. Make two keyframes in the movie clip, one keyframe where the top of the text is far down. The other keyframe the top of the text is far up.

Step 4. Apply Motion Tweening between these frames.

Step 5. Go to the normal Scene Editor by pressing CTRL + E. Name the movie clip "scrolltext" by selecting properties for the clip.

Step 6. Now make two buttons. One for scroll up, one for scroll down.

Step 7. Give the Scrolll UP button this action:

Begin Tell Target [scrolltext]
Goto and Stop [next frame]
End Tell Target

Step 8. Give the Scrolll DOWN button this action:

Begin Tell Target [scrolltext]
Goto and Stop [previous frame]
End Tell Target

That should do the trick!

Testing the final product
We now have a finished module that is both draggable and collapsible! If you have the Flash 4 player installed, you can play with it yourself below (Note: the Flash 3 player will show all of the movie clip that it can, but since it does not include dragging capabilities, that function will not work.):

Try the following:
1. Click and drag the module by it's grip handle at the top. Drop the module wherever you like.
2. Double-click on the grip handle to close the module. Double-click a second time to open it again.
